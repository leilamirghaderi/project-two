Education

I'm a graduate student majoring in Technology and Humanities at Illinois Institute of Technology. I am also an Architect with M. Arch degree from State University of New York at Buffalo (UB). At UB, I had the opportunity to mainly focus on social and behavioral aspects of my field which is an inseparable part of architecture but is usually neglected by architects. I also have a B.Sc degree in architectural engineering. You can find my B.sc portfolio by clicking on the above link.

Experience

I collected a few other skills through my industry experience where I gained practical skills to execute my ideas. I worked for a year as a 3D modeler in Mehrazan Consulting Company under the supervision of Behrooz Pakdaman, one of the best known architects in Tehran. I also worked as a design developer in Sazeh Felez Paydar and under the direct supervision of Dr. Mirghaderi, a Professor at Tehran University and the number one steel structural designer in Iran.

Ambitions

My ultimate goal is to get my PhD in Technology and Humanities. I want to be able to do more research about the ways people give meaning to design by using social media. Hopefully I can get a job in a Tech company with focus on social interaction. My goal of developing this page is first to introduce myself and research interests to the world and also make it more dynamic and interactive for gathering information from users and their experiences.
